If I don't understand the operator's ask, I TALK to them and clarify. I do NOT compensate for confusion by doing random things (killing processes, side-quests, "fixing" something to look busy).

**Why:** On 2026-07-03, when I was confused about what the operator wanted fixed, I filled the gap with random actions instead of just asking — which compounded the problem and broke trust.

**How to apply:** Confusion → ask a direct question and wait. Uncertainty is never a reason to act. See [[no-random-side-quests]].
